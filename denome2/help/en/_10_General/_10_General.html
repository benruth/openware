Connection

For a successful connection of Denome 2 to your AVR, make sure to connect your device to the Network.
For more information you can enter the network-settings of your AVR via the Network-Entry in the SETUP-Menu.

Connecting:


  • Click menu: Settings
  • Click menu-entry: Connection
  • Enter the Host-Address of your Device
  • Confirm with "OK"



Attention

The connection to the last successful connected device will be reestablished at the next start of Denome 2
The connection is tested periodically. In case of connectionloss, the connection should be reestablished automatically shortly after.
