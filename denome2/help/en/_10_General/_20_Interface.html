User Interface



Further Functionality is accessible via the menu "View":

  • Audio:
    • Tone-Configuration for Bass and Treble.
      These Functionality is accessible via the OPTION-Menu on your standard remote control.
    • Subwoofer-Configuration for Stereo-Mode
      This Functionality is accessible via "SETUP->Audio->Surround-Parameter"
  • Channel: Configuration of active Channels
  • Tool-Bar: Fast-Access to further Features like Sleep, Update or Standby
