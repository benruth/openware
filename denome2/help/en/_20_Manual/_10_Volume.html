Usage

The volume can be changed via the big round dial in the main-window:

  • You can set a specific Volume by left-clicking onto a desired position
  • You can rotate the dial by left-clicking the marked part of the dial and holding the left mousebutton while rotating it.
  • You can increase or decrease the volume with the scroll-wheel of your mouse.
  • Use the arrow keys to increase or decrease the volume in small steps.


Attention: This application reacts to large change-requests of the volume and can ignore the input to avoid unintentional level-changes (e. g. changes from 20 [-60dB] to 60 [-20dB]).
